Formmail sample

This sample form is preconfigured to use Formmail.pl on your account, a universal CGI program and WWW form to Email gateway. Submitting this form will send the information via email to your administrator account's email address jeresmith@jeresmith.com. Feel free to copy this form for use into your web pages. You can add/subtract any number of fields that you wish.
